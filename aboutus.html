Our Story

CO Founder Scarlett


Scarlett always had a passion for baking desserts. Every morning she would prepare her kitchen for chaos when she's about to learn a new recipe. There would be a flour feast on her counter as the other ingredients are piled in one corner. Scarlett loved baking for her friends as she wanted them to enjoy the warmth of food. With the help of her friends and their support, she opened Fuwa Fuwa Bakery. "The smiles on people's faces always bring me joy."
